Cake Tips and Techniques

Professinal Tips

When baking a cheesecake, it is important not to over bake it. You only want to bake it until the middle has slight wiggle, not until it is rock solid.

It is important that you use a water bath, discussed at the right, to ensure even baking of the cheesecake.

To create a water bath , use a pan that will allow you to fill it with boiling water that 29 goes halfway up the spring form pan in which the cake is placed.
